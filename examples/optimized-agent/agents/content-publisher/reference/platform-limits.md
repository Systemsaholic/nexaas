# Platform Rate Limits & Guidelines

## Posting Limits

| Platform | Daily Max | Hourly Max | Min Spacing |
|----------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Facebook | 5 | 2 | 2 hours |
| Instagram | 5 | 2 | 3 hours |
| LinkedIn | 3 | 1 | 4 hours |
| Twitter | 10 | 3 | 30 minutes |
| TikTok | 3 | 1 | 4 hours |

## Content Limits

| Platform | Text Limit | Hashtags | Media |
|----------|------------|----------|-------|
| Facebook | 63,206 | No limit | 10 images or 1 video |
| Instagram | 2,200 | 30 max | 10 images or 1 video |
| LinkedIn | 3,000 | 3-5 recommended | 9 images or 1 video |
| Twitter | 280 | 2-3 recommended | 4 images or 1 video |
| TikTok | 2,200 | 4-5 recommended | 1 video required |

## Best Practices

### Optimal Posting Times

| Platform | Best Times (Local) |
|----------|-------------------|
| Facebook | 9 AM, 1 PM, 4 PM |
| Instagram | 11 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM |
| LinkedIn | 8 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM |
| Twitter | 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM |

### Cross-Posting Rules

- Never include Twitter in cross-platform posts (280 char limit)
- LinkedIn needs professional tone adjustment
- TikTok requires native video, never cross-post

## Rate Limit Recovery

When hitting rate limits:

1. Log the limit hit with timestamp
2. Calculate reset time
3. Queue remaining posts for later
4. Alert if queued posts exceed 10
