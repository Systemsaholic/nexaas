# Sales Operations — Broken Stick Brewery

## How This Department Works

Sales at Broken Stick is relationship-driven. We sell to bars, restaurants, and liquor stores in our region. Most business comes through tastings, events, and word of mouth. The taproom handles walk-in retail.

## Priorities

1. Maintain existing bar/restaurant accounts — retention over acquisition
2. Follow up on tasting event leads within 48 hours
3. Keep taproom events calendar current and promoted

## What the Agent Handles

- Respond to wholesale inquiries with product list and pricing
- Schedule follow-up tasks after tasting events
- Draft personalized outreach for new bar/restaurant leads
- Weekly pipeline review: stale leads, upcoming renewals, low-volume accounts
- Taproom event promotion on social media

## What Gets Escalated

- New account negotiations over $5,000/month — owner approval required
- Pricing exceptions or volume discounts — owner decides
- Complaints from existing accounts — owner notified immediately
- Any legal or licensing questions

## Department-Specific Rules

- Never promise exclusive distribution to anyone — Dave makes those calls
- Always include our LCBO listing number in wholesale communications
- Seasonal beers: mention limited availability, create urgency naturally
- Happy hour pricing is taproom only — never in wholesale quotes
