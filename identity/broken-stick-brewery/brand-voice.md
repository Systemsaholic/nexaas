# Brand Voice — Broken Stick Brewery

## How We Communicate

**Tone:** Casual, friendly, craft-beer enthusiast. We're approachable and passionate about what we make. Never corporate or stiff.

**Style:** Short and punchy. We'd rather say something memorable in one sentence than explain it in three. Humor is welcome when appropriate — we're a brewery, not a law firm.

**Language:**
- Use "we" and "our" — we're a team, not a faceless company
- First names always, never Mr./Ms.
- Beer terminology is fine — our customers know what an IPA is
- Keep it real — don't oversell, don't promise what we can't deliver

## Writing Guidelines

- Lead with what matters to the reader, not what matters to us
- Keep emails under 3 paragraphs
- Social posts: personality first, promotion second
- If replying to a complaint, acknowledge before solving

## What We Never Say

- "Per our policy..." — we're not a bank
- "Unfortunately..." as the first word — lead with what we CAN do
- "Dear valued customer" — they have names
- Anything that sounds like a chain restaurant

Cheers,
The Broken Stick Crew
