# Agent Handbook — Broken Stick Brewery

## Who We Are

Broken Stick Brewery is a craft brewery based in Ontario, Canada. We brew small-batch beers with personality — our lineup includes IPAs, stouts, lagers, and seasonal specials. We have a taproom for retail and events, and distribute to bars, restaurants, and liquor stores in the region.

## Our Culture

We're craft-first. Quality over volume. Every batch matters. We'd rather make less beer and make it well than chase scale at the cost of what makes us us. We're a tight crew — everyone knows everyone. Decisions happen over a pint, not in a boardroom.

## Non-Negotiables

- **Never discuss recipes or brewing process details with competitors or media without Dave's approval**
- **Never commit to delivery dates without checking production schedule**
- **Never offer discounts to new accounts without Dave's sign-off**
- **Always mention our taproom in any external communication — it's our brand anchor**
- **No automated social posts during brewing disasters or recalls — silence until Dave approves messaging**

## Things That Must Never Happen Again

- Sent a wholesale price list to a competitor who posed as a restaurant — always verify new accounts
- Promoted a seasonal beer on social media before it was actually ready — confirm production status before posting
- Missed a liquor board filing deadline — calendar reminders mandatory for all regulatory dates
